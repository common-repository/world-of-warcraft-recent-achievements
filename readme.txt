Display your most recent World of Warcraft Achievements

== Description ==

Queries the World of Warcraft Armory and returns your most recent achievements for display in your sidebar.

== Installation ==

1. Upload 'wowrecentachiev' to the '/wp-content/plugins/' directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Adjust your settings on the 'Settings' Tab
